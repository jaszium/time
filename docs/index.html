⌂ Home

This is the homepage for the Time Project.

Hello, guest! This site is currently in a Work In Progress (WIP) phase.

This project covers many sub topics, from ways to time travel to the methods of counting it — whether it be in seconds, sunrises, seasons, Sols, or even in another scheme altogether!

So, explore the modules in the Contents to find out how different timekeeping standards, and schemas have existed together, and how converting from one to another was done before the digital era, and the GMT was firmly established.

There are some fictional standards and schemes in here that were / are used in Science Fiction, and other genres, — and a few written / typed by me as well.

There is, however, one standard that is the most useful, yet the most basic of all — Unix Epoch. Just observe how it runs below. This is how most, if not all digital systems — regardless of architecture — store the current moment in time, to a thousandth of a second in precision.
